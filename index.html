Action Movies

Movies in the action genre are fast-paced and include a lot of action like fight scenes, chase scenes, and slow-motion shots.They can feature superheroes, martial arts, or exciting stunts The excitement from these action movies is so electrifying, that we would not even be aware of our popcorn or soft drinks vanishing.

Comedy Movies

Comedy is a genre of film that uses humor as a driving force,The films in this genre center around a comedic premise usually putting someone in a challenging,amusing,or humorous situation they’re not prepared to handle.Good comedy movies are less about making constant jokes and more about presenting a universally relatable, real-life story with complex characters who learn an important lesson

Horror Movies

Horror films feature elements that leave people with an overwhelming sense of fear and dread Horror movies often include serial killers or monsters as persistent, evil antagonists to play on viewers’ fears or nightmares Audiences who love the horror genre seek out these movies specifically for the adrenaline rush produced by ghosts, gore, monsters, and jump scares

Sci-Fi Movies

Sci-fi genre builds worlds and alternate realities filled with imagined elements that don't don’t exist in the real world. Science fiction spans a wide range of themes that often explore time travel, space travel, are set in the future and deal with the consequences of technological and scientific advances.
