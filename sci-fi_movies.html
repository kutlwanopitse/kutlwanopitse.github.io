Sci-Fi Movies

The Matrix (1999)

Summary: Set in the 22nd century, The Matrix tells the story of a computer hacker who joins a group of underground insurgents fighting the vast and powerful computers who now rule the earth.

Director: Lana Wachowski, Lilly Wachowski

Cast: Keanu Reeve, Marcus Chong, Rowan Witt, Robert Taylor, Paul Goddard, Janaya Pender, Laurence Fishbourne

Tenet (2020)

Summary: Armed with only one word - Tenet - and fighting for the survival of the entire world, the Protagonist journeys through a twilight world of international espionage on a mission that will unfold in something beyond real time.

Director: Christopher Nolan

Cast: Jess Weber, Rich Ceraulo, Dimple Kapadia, Andrew Howard, Laurie Shepherd

The Island (2005)

Summary: A man living in a futuristic sterile colony begins to question his circumscribed existence when his friend is chosen to go to the Island, the last uncontaminated place on earth.

Director: Michael Bay

Cast: Ewan McGregor, Scarlett Johansson, Djimon Hounsou, Sean Bean, Steve Buscemi, Michael Clarke Duncan, Ethan Phillips, Brian Stepanek, Noa Tishby, Siobhan Flynn,Troy Blendell, Jamie McBride, Kevin McCorkle, Gary Nickens, Kathleen Rose Perkins.
